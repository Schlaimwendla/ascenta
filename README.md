# 🦾👓 Ascenta – Guiding your Vision  

Welcome to **Ascenta** – a smart glasses project designed to **support blind and visually impaired people** in their daily lives.  
This is our **diploma thesis** project, powered by the **Arduino Nicla Vision board** and connected to a mobile app for seamless interaction.  

---

## 🌟 Project Idea  
Ascenta aims to make the world more accessible by combining **computer vision**, **edge AI**, and **wearable technology**:  

- 👓 **Smart Glasses** equipped with the **Nicla Vision** board  
- 📱 **Mobile App** connection for additional features and user control  
- 🧭 Guidance and assistance in daily life activities  
- ❤️ Designed with empathy and inclusivity at its core  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Hardware:** Arduino Nicla Vision (Edge AI, camera, sensors)  
- **Software:** MicroPython / Arduino IDE / Android App  
- **Connectivity:** WiFi - TCP for glasses ↔ app communication  
- **Features in progress:**  
  - Navigation hints  
  - Daily reminders & assistance via app  

---

## 🚀 Vision  
We believe technology should **remove barriers** – not create them.  
With Ascenta, our goal is to bring **independence, safety, and confidence** to visually impaired individuals.  

---

## 📲 About the App  
The companion app serves as a **hub for communication** between the smart glasses and the user:  
- Live feedback  
- Configurable guidance modes  
- Daily assistant (plans, reminders, etc.)
